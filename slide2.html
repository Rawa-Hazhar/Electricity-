2.2 Electrical Safety in Domestic Appliances

Understanding essential protection methods in electrical devices

I

Insulation

Non-conducting material covering electrical components

  • Prevents direct contact with live parts
  • Made from rubber or plastic materials
  • Must be regularly checked for damage
Critical Safety Feature
D

Double Insulation

Two layers of insulating material for extra protection

  • No need for earth wire
  • Common in portable appliances
  • Marked with square-within-square symbol
Enhanced Protection
E

Earthing

Safety connection to ground

  • Diverts dangerous currents safely
  • Green/Yellow wire in plugs
  • Connected to metal casings
Essential Safety
F

Fuses

Sacrificial safety device

  • Melts when current exceeds rating
  • Must be replaced after blowing
  • Different ratings for different appliances
